CAR MAINTENANCE

What is this app all about?

This application was runned in case to help you with keeping all your car costs just in one place. When you will start using it, you'll admit, that it is very easy to use and interruct with. In particular, main delight is in its comfortable and understandable user interface: all available options of improvment are devided in categories to make it possible for users to better navigate throughout all the application. Each of your car's improvment will convert into new note in app - by the way, it looks very similar with usual todo sheet - wich also is good news as it will not be too tricky for you to use it - with its own date of change, total cost, as far as mileages, and as a nice addition to existing convinient things. This application contains possibility to easily add all needed notes. Also in case to increase user experience there several things were added: options for changing the metric system, currency, theme and animations. At the moment, the development of warehouse functionality is implied, so in the near future you can personally appreciate the beauty of the convenience of this functionality

The first convinient thing is that you will keep all the costs and improvtments of your car in one simple place - which, undeniable, is very important in case not to lose sight of any changes

The second moment - user-friendly interface: it will not take a lot of your time to get acquainted with app - all available features intuitively clear

And third - but not less important - is that your app will look just like as you want it to look. Changing themes, currencies, animations and so on - it depends on you and your mood
